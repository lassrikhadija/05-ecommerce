Manifesto

We believe less, but better is the future of fine jewelry.

No disposable seasonal collections. No dormant stock, no endless production runs. Every Aurélia piece is designed to outlast fashion cycles and generations — and crafted to last longer than those who wear it.

Our workshop works exclusively with RJC-certified 18K gold and lab-grown stones identical to mined stones (composition, hardness, brilliance) — without the human or environmental cost.

Read the full manifesto

Certified 18K gold

100% recycled gold certified by the Responsible Jewellery Council, traceable from mine to your piece.

Lab-grown stones

Diamonds, emeralds and rubies grown in laboratories, IGI-certified. Identical, only better.

Lifetime warranty

Polishing, resizing, engraving: your piece stays as new, forever.

Signature case

Every piece arrives in its marquetry wooden case, ready to be gifted.

Workshop · Old Montréal

From sketch to the hand that wears it.

Our jewelers sketch, sculpt wax, cast gold and set every stone in the same workshop on Saint-Paul East. From first sketch to signed case: twenty-two steps, six pairs of hands, zero middlemen.

By appointment, we also take on bespoke pieces: transforming a family heirloom, designing an engagement ring, recasting forgotten jewelry.

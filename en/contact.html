Frequently asked

Everything you want to know before buying.

Are your lab-grown diamonds real diamonds?

Yes. Identical chemical composition, hardness (10 on the Mohs scale) and brilliance. The only difference is their origin: a controlled laboratory in India or the United States, rather than a mine. All our diamonds come with an IGI certificate specifying carat, color, clarity and cut.

What are the production lead times?

In-stock pieces from the collection: 2 to 4 business days, shipped from Montréal.
Simple personalization (engraving, custom sizing): 7 to 10 days.
Full bespoke: 6 to 10 weeks, across three appointments.

Can I resize my ring?

Yes — free during the first year after purchase. After that, by quote: $50 to $150 depending on complexity (settings, interior engraving). Service in-store or via secure mail (we provide the label).

Do you ship internationally?

For now: Canada and United States only. Rates and times shown at checkout. All shipments are insured and require a signature on delivery.

How does the lifetime warranty work?

All manufacturing defects are covered for life: setting, soldering, clasp. Polishing is offered every 12 months in store. Impacts, accidental oxidation and normal wear are not covered but repaired at cost.

Can I trade in my old gold for a bespoke piece?

Yes, and it's one of the most beautiful forms of transmission. We test your gold, melt it down and recycle it into the piece you'll wear. Allow about 2 additional weeks. Any missing weight is billed at the gold price on the melt date.
